    • Homemade Bread Recipe

    Step 1

    Dissolve the yeast in warm water in a large mixing bowl with a pinch of sugar. When the yeast bubbles add the sugar, salt, oil, and 4 cups of flour, and stir till smooth.

    Step 2

    Add the rest of the flour, 1/2 cup at a time. The dough should slightly stick to your fingers when you touch it. Knead dough for 5-7 minutes and then form it into a ball.

    Step 3

    Place the kneaded dough into a lightly oiled bowl and turn it so that all sides are coated. Then cover it with plastic wrap and let the dough rise for 1 hour.

    Step 4

    After the dough has risen for 1 hour divide it in half. Roll each half into a long cyclinder to fit into your bread pan.

    Step 5

    Place the dough into lightly greased bread pans with the seam facing down and cover with plastic wrap again. Let the dough rise for another hour.

    Step 6

    Preheat the oven to 375 degrees and bake for 30-35 minutes or until the loaves are golden brown and sound hollow to the touch. Brush them with melted butter and let cool before slicing.
